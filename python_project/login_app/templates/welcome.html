About:

Here in Palestine, when you're bored and looking for something to do, the usual things people everywhere else may think of, like going to the cinema, or going to the beach, are usually out of reach. Also, in each city, there are many hidden gems, and places only people from the city know about. Our idea is to provide a platform for Palestinians to share and learn about different events in their area, or an area they are visiting, and to get new ideas about activities they can do anytime.
